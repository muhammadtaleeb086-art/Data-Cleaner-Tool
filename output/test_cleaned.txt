Data science is one of the most important fields in today's world companies
generate huge amount of data every single day but this data is often
unstructured and contains many errors which makes it hard for analysts to
perform proper analysis machine learning algorithms require clean data otherwise
predictions can become inaccurate and models might fail to generalize on unseen
data this leads to poor decision making in business environments which can cause
financial losses and reduce operational efficiency therefore data cleaning is a
very important step before applying any machine learning model python is widely
used for data analysis and preprocessing tasks libraries like pandas numpy and
matplotlib help developers to handle messy datasets and convert them into
structured formats artificial intelligence systems depend heavily on clean and
well formatted data to function effectively and produce reliable results in real
world applications data quality plays a crucial role in ensuring the success of
predictive analytics systems.
